Ingredients:
  - 3/4 cup chia seeds
  - 2 cups almond milk
  - 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  - 3- 4 pitted dates
  - Pinch of salt

Directions:
 1. Mix the ingredients and let them rest for a few moments. Stir the mix well with a fork every five minutes or so, until the chia seeds plump up and the pudding resembles tapioca pudding.
